"Travel is fatal to prejudice, bigotry, and narrow-mindedness, and many of our people need it sorely on these accounts. Broad, wholesome, charitable views of men and things cannot be acquired by vegetating in one little corner of the earth all one's lifetime." -Mark Twain


I write about travel to convey both the complex challenges and beauties of humanity. I have always said that if we each had a friend from every country, there would be a lot less war, and so much more understanding. Governments are not people. People are more diverse, more empathic, and more vulnerable. You may dislike the policies of certain countries, but I guarantee that you could make a friend any country. Through this blog, I will introduce you to the friends I make traveling; then, hopefully, you can imagine making your own, imagine a more connected, more understanding world.
